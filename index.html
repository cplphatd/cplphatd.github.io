About

Hi, my name's David! I am a full-stack developer based in San Antonio. Thanks for visiting my site.

My dad was in the Army when I was growing up, so I have lived in lots of places, but I consider San Antonio my hometown. I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Biology at the University of Texas Austin in 2008. Before graduating I decided to enter the Navy's Nuclear Power Officer Candidate and was accepted.

After graduation I went to Officer Candidate School in Newport, Rhode Island and earned my commission as an officer in the Navy. I spent 5 years in the Navy and travelled to places like Japan, Singapore, Thailand, Brunei, and the Philippines. The technical aspect of the job and the opportunity to use problem solving skills made it enjoyable, but I decided to separate and pursue a MBA.

I was accepted into Texas State University's Master of Business Administration program. Some courses in the program required a program called R Project that was used for statistical analysis. The syntax in R Project was like a coding language and I really enjoyed using it. This is where my love of code began to develop. I graduated from Texas State University with a MBA in International Business in 2016. During the job hunt, I noticed a lot of financial analyst positions requiring experience with Java and mySQL, so I decided to gain these skills and become a better candidate.

I enrolled at Codeup with the intent to pick up coding skills to make me a better candidate for jobs in the financial industry, but enjoyed coding so much that I decided to pursue software development as a career. Codeup taught me the skills to be a full-stack developer, but I like to work with Java, Spring, mySQL, and JavaScript. I really enjoy the opportunity to build applications that help people solve their problems and always aim to improve my code.

Outside of work I am an avid video game fan, love to travel and practice photography, cook, ride road and mountain bikes, run races, and cheer for the Cowboys and Spurs.
